Recieve Updates Regarding Finn Maps?

Your information will be stored on a secure server and never shared with anyone. if you opt for notifications you will recieve text messages and/or emails when Finn is going to visit somewhere and when any major changes happen to Finn Maps. Your name is not required unless you would like to recieve a personalized notification (e.g. Hi Karen!). If this ever changes you will be notified and given the option to opt out. Currently you cannot remove yourself from the notification list through the application but email cvricella2@outlook.com with "opt out" in the subject line and your contact info in the body and I will remove you. A future update will provide this capability through the application.
